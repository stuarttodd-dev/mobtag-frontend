Esso Prefab

The very first client who recognised the benefit of the Mobtag system, ESSCO Prefab specialise in the provision of Prefab Utility Cupboards for Heat Networks.

With communal heating in apartments on the rise, many developers are choosing to install prefabricated utility cupboards to reduce cost and risk. This also allows them to deliver projects ahead of schedule.

Our utility cupboards are supplied fully fitted, pre-tested and ready to install on-site.

Typical Components:

  • Heat Interface Unit
  • Cooling Interface Unit (Optional)
  • Mechanical Ventilation and Heat Recovery Unit
  • Water Meter Assembly
  • All associated piping and electricals

Essco cupboards are built bespoke to project design requirements and utilise the system to track construction status, critical test documentation, and handover manuals to clients on-site.

With full system traceability ensuring ISO9001 compliance, we maintain high standards while digitising the product for clients.
